\begin{abstract}
We study input-side preprocessing for text-rich images under matched visual budgets.
BOPS significantly improves OCR word recall over aggressive spatial resizing, but question-agnostic OCR-guided patch selection does not reliably transfer to DocVQA/VLM performance because selected patches often miss answer-bearing regions.
\end{abstract}
